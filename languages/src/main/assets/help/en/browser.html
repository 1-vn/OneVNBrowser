Download action

You can change the behavior when downloading files etc.

Do nothing
Ignore the download request.
Auto
In the case of a video playback request or the like, the URL is opened by another application, and otherwise, a download dialog is displayed.
Always download
Always display download dialog.
Always open
Always open the URL in another application.
Share
Always share URLs.
Select
Every time you receive a download request, select which behavior to open, download, and share.
